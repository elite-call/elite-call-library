About the Library

The Elite Call Library renders every rep's monthly performance as a collectible trading card. Metrics update weekly from Trellus and the dialer.

Cards stay in progress until the month closes. When grades lock, the edition moves to the Archive.

Rarity is earned. Better stats increase your odds — but the final draw has an element of chance.
