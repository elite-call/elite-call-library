About the Library
How It Works

The Elite Call Library converts every rep's monthly performance data into a collectible trading card. Each metric on the card — dials, leads per hour, close rate, ACC, Sched %, and Gold Rate — is pulled automatically from Trellus and the dialer every week.

Cards stay in progress until the four-week month closes. When grades lock, the edition is finalized and moved to the Archive tab in the Card Vault. Past editions are never erased — every rep has a permanent record.

Rarity is earned, not assigned. Better stats increase your odds of pulling a higher-tier card — but the final draw has an element of chance, just like opening a real pack.
